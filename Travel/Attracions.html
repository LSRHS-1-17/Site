Welcome to Miami!

  • There are many things to do in Miami.
  • One option is to go to American Airlines Arena to watch the basketball team, the Miami Heat play.
  • Also, you can take a helicopter tour around Miami. On your trip you will see many sights such as Sun Life Stadium, the city of Aventura, Gulfstream Park Racing and Casino, plus Hallandale Beach, Golden Beach, Sunny Isles Beach and much more.
  • Finally, you can go to Zoo Miami. Zoo Miami consists of 340 developed acres with more than 3,000 animals and over 1,200 plants and trees.
